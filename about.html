About The Learning Village

The Learning Village is the first Specialty Program in the Central Valley. The goal of this Specialty Program is to use project based learning (PBL) to tackle real-world issues. We also consider ourselves Makers and Doers. We make thing and we do things in the world to learn and to be productive citizens.

Mission: The Learning Village is a nonprofit dedicated to supporting homeschooling families through innovative education and community building.

Vision: The Learning Village's mission is to foster an inclusive educational community through dynamic project based learning.

Our Team

Karin Livingstone
Director, Board Member

Karin Livingstone, homeschooling mother of 2, has a Women's Studies degree from University of California Santa Cruz. Community organizer and social activist, Karin has been involved with La Leche League, Homeschool Association of California, Planned Parenthood for 15+ years. She has worked as a Director of Education since 2010, and has proudly unschooled with her own family since 2001. “I am exhilarated to be a part of The Learning Village. Bringing our students’ interests to life, with hands-on lessons and real-world experiences, is the cornerstone of our philosophy at TLV. I see TLV as a true community collaboration with such exciting potential, both for our families and the world at large.”
