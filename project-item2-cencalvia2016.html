Open Streets in Fresno

Over 50 cities in the US already temporarily turn commercial streets into “paved parks” as a part of the Open Streets movement. Residents are invited to bike, walk, skate, take classes in yoga, zumba, hula hooping, bicycle safety and repair, or martial arts along the streets and sidewalks without car traffic.

TLV students created a kinetic sculpture called the Unnecessarily High Fist Bump based loosely on the Unnecessarily High Five.

Project Details

  • Organized by the Fresno County Bicycle Coalition.
